Strategy Math

  • Bayesian: P(H|D) = P(D|H)·P(H) / P(D)
  • Edge: EV = p̂ − p_market
  • Kelly: f* = EV / (p̂(1−p̂))
  • LMSR: p(q) = e^{q/b} / Σe^{qⱼ/b}
